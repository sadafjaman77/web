Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Large PDF Performance

My PDF Reader is optimized for heavy documents. For files larger than 100MB, the app automatically enables a "Performance Mode" which skips thumbnail generation to ensure smooth scrolling and prevent crashes on older devices.

Missing Files? Use Rescan

If you recently downloaded a PDF and it doesn't appear in the list, tap the Rescan button in the top toolbar. This forces the app to re-index your device storage to find new documents instantly.

Why "Manage Storage"?

Android requires the Manage External Storage permission so we can find PDF files located anywhere on your device. We use this strictly locally to display your library. No files are ever sent to the cloud.
